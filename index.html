Company Bio

We are a team of experts with many years of experience on Maui. We can design or transform the pond of your dreams!

Owner, Scott Jonke, has lived on Maui for over 23 years. He owned an aquarium business in Los Angeles in the late 80's and moved to Maui in 1991 after falling in love with it. He was fortunate enough to land a great job running an exlusive vacation rental home in Wailea. The home had huge waterfalls and koi ponds, but Scott was never impressed with the water quality or the appearance. This was when he decided to focus his profession on improving fresh water aquatics.

Scott's specialty is in water quality. He is also an expert in salt water husbandry. He consults for several wholesale companies. If you are looking for your own personal aquatic expert or if you have any questions regarding your water feature please call Scott at (808) 280-7442 for a free initial consultation.
